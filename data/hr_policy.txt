# Nexus-Mind HR Policy Manual

## 1. Zero Tolerance Harassment Policy
Nexus-Mind is committed to providing a safe workspace. We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment of any kind.
- **Reporting:** Any incidents should be immediately reported to hr@nexus.com or via the anonymous whistleblower portal.
- **Consequences:** Substantiated claims typically result in immediate termination.

## 2. Remote Work & Async Communication
- We are a remote-first company.
- **Core Hours:** All employees are expected to be available between 10 AM and 2 PM EST for synchronous meetings.
- **Slack Etiquette:** Please use threads for discussions. Do not use @channel unless it is an emergency (system down).

## 3. Leave & Time Off
- **PTO:** Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off per year.
- **Sick Leave:** 5 days of sick leave are provided.
- **Maternity/Paternity:** 12 weeks of paid leave for primary caregivers.
